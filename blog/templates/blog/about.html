About Us

Have you ever experienced the feeling of pure triumph as you glide into the perfect parking space and then wished you had someone to share it with? Well this blog is for you.

We here at Snack-bar Parking want to celebrate the victories on the parking battlefield. The magical spot equidistant to the entrance and the trolley bay. The only undercover space on a rainy day. The park right outside the pizza shop just as your pizza is coming out of the oven. We are by no means experts in parking and we’re certainly not representatives from the parking industry; we’re just a little obsessed.

You’ll find lots of posts about our personal snack-bar parks (the best park to suit your needs) as well as car park reviews, tips about how to take a little stress out of your daily parking routine and some history about this strange custom we all suffer through.

Don’t forget to @ us with your own snack-bar wins on Twitter or check out our Instagram and Pintrest where we share beautiful parking spots and parking inspiration.

Peace, love and may we never fight over the same parking space!
